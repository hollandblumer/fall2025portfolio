Overview

Collwick Capital, LLC (“Collwick”) is an independent, privately owned alternative investment firm based in Charlotte, North Carolina. Since our founding in 2010, Collwick has successfully managed a diversified, multi-strategy fund of hedge funds portfolio. The fund seeks to achieve capital preservation and strong risk-adjusted returns for our investors.
